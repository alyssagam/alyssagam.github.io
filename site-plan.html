Overview

Purpose

Dry Oar wishes to provide unique opportunities to guests in the beautiful outdoors. We strive to provide a safe environment for learning and growth. We want to challenge guests to be out of their comfort zones in a fun and exciting adventure.

Audience

The target audience is between 18-50 year old adults who love the outdoors and seeks adventures.
