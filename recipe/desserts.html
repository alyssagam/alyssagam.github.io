Fruit Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 square butter
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp soda
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 8oz cream cheese, softened
  • One 8oz Cool Whip or whipped cream
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Directions

To make the crust, cream together sugar, butter, eggs. Add and mix salt, baking powder, and soda. Spread on greased cookie sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 to 15 minutes.

To make the frosting, mix cream cheese, whipped cream, sugar, and vanilla. Spread on top of the crust. Add on desired fruit such as strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, kiwis, bananas, mandarin oranges, etc.

Banana Drop Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup bananas (2 bananas mashed)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2/3 cup shortening
  • ½ tsp vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 ½ cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips or butterscotch chips

Directions

Mix bananas and sugar first and let sit a minute. Mix rest of the ingredients together. Bake at 400 degrees for 10 – 12 minutes.

Lollipop Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg cake mix (rainbow chip – suggested)
  • ¾ cup water
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tub frosting or homemade frosting
  • 20 – 24 wooden sticks

Directions

Heat oven to 375 degrees. Beat dry cake mix and eggs on low speed for 30 seconds. Beat on high speed 2 minutes. Drop dough by rounded Tbsp 3 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheet. Insert wooden stick 1 ½ inches into edge of dough. Bake 8 – 11 minutes or until puffed and almost no indentation remains when touched. Cool 1 minute. Remove from cookie sheet. Cool on wire rack. Frost and decorate.

Homemade Oreo Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg Devils Food cake mix
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup oil
  • 3 oz cream cheese, softened
  • ¼ cup butter or margarine, softened
  • 2 to 3 cups powdered sugar
  • ¼ tsp vanilla

Directions

In a mixing bowl, beat the cake mix, eggs, and oil. Roll the dough into 1 inch balls. Place on an ungreased cookie sheet and flatten them slightly. Bake at 350 degrees for 8 to 10 minutes. Let cool.

In another bowl, beat the cream cheese and butter. Add the sugar and vanilla. Mix until smooth. Frost half of the cookies on the bottoms and place the remaining cookies on top to make sandwiches for the Homemade Oreos.

Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 small can pumpkin
  • Bag of chocolate chips
  • 1 spice cake mix

Directions

Mix pumpkin and spice cake. Add chocolate chips. Drop by spoonful on baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes.

Whatchamacallits

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup karo syrup
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 5 cups rice krispies
  • Chocolate chips and/or butterscotch chips

Directions

Bring sugar, karo syrup to a boil. Remove from heat. Add peanut butter. Add to rice krispies. Press into a greased 9 x 13 pan. Melt chocolate and/or butterscotch chips and spread on top.

Lunch Lady Peanut Butter Bars

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, firmly packed
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/2 cups peanut butter (divided)
  • 2 cups oatmeal (not quick)
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt

Directions

Cream sugar, brown sugar, and butter together. Add the vanilla, eggs, and 1 cup peanut butter and combine. Add the remaining ingredients and mix together. Spread dough on a greased jelly roll pan (18x13x1"). Make at 350 degrees for 15 minutes (until golden brown).

While hot spread the other 1 1/2 cups peanut butter on top. Drop all over top by little spoonsful. It will soften (melt) and make it easier to spread. Then let cool so that the peanut butter has become solid again. Spread your favorite chocolate frosting on top and cut into bars.

Favorite Chocolate Frosting

Ingredients

  • 1 stick (1/2 cup) margarine, softened
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1-2 tsp vanilla (depending on your taste)
  • 3 Tablespoons cocoa (I have never really exactly measured)
  • 2-3 cups of powdered sugar

Directions

Mix all ingredients with a hand mixer until smooth.
