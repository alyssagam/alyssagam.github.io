Welcome to Millennial Meals!

Our Mission

Do you find it difficult to make tasty, healthy meals for you and your family every day? Whether you are a college student or a busy parent, life gets busy and leaves little time for making a homecooked dinner. We live in a fast pace world with many options of Fast Food Restaurants for our meals. Being constantly on the run is difficult to make a delicious, easy, and nutritious meal for you and your family or roommates.
My website, Millennial Meals, was inspired by my sister who recently finished college and become a mother to twins. Trying to balance their schedule and making healthy meals was difficult until she found recipes that made delicious meals in a short time. She wanted a platform to share these meals with other busy students and parents but also options for others to share their own recipe.
My site will have many recipes including ones my sister has loved and a page for others to post their own recipes to be added to the growing list of quick and delicious meals.

We want your help!
