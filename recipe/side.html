Bowtie Salad

Ingredients

  • 6 cups cooked chicken, cut into pieces
  • 6 sups cooked bowtie noodles
  • 4 tsp onion, grated
  • 16 oz coleslaw dressing
  • 2 cups mayonnaise, miracle whip, or yogurt
  • 2 cups celery, diced
  • 4 cups red grapes, cut in half
  • 4 cups apples, unpeeled, diced
  • 2 cans drained pineapple tidbits (20 oz)
  • Chopped almonds or pecans, optional
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp pepper
  • 2 dashes thyme
  • 2 pinches ginger

Directions

Serves a big group, so half recipe if for smaller group. Mix together in large bowl. It is best chilled before serving.

Submitted by

Heather Gamble. "This is a favorite at family BBQ dinners."

Rainbow Antipasto Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 oz) package rainbow twirl pasta
  • ½ lb ham, cubed
  • ½ lb Provolone cheese, cubed
  • 1 ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ¾ cup pitted black olives, chopped
  • ¾ cup green pepper, cut in strips
  • 1 (2 oz) jar sliced pimento, drained
  • 1 cup Italian dressing

Directions

Cook pasta according to package directions, drain and cool. In a large bowl, toss cooked pasta with the remaining ingredients. Chill, stirring occasionally. It is best chilled several hours or overnight.

Taco Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 package mild taco seasoning mix
  • 16 oz can cut corn
  • 16 oz can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 28 oz can stewed tomatoes
  • 8 oz can tomato sauce

Directions

Brown ground beef. Drain. Saute onion; add to ground beef. Stir in taco seasoning, corn, kidney beans, stewed tomatoes, and tomato sauce. Simmer for 20 – 30 minutes. Serve topped with tortilla chips and grated cheese. Serves 8.

Ham and Cheese Slider Appetizers

Ingredients

  • 1 stick butter
  • 2 tablespoon dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds (optional)
  • 2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon grated onion
  • 8 ounces thinly sliced ham
  • 2 (6 ounce ) packages Swiss cheese
  • 2 packages rolls (in tin pans)

Directions

Combine butter, mustard, poppy seeds, Worcestershire, and onion into a small sauce pan and heat until butter is melted. While that is heating through, remove rolls from pan and slice in half and remove the top, like a sheet.

Pour 1/4 of butter mixture on bottom layer of rolls. Layer ham and cheese, then top with another 1/4 of the butter mixture, then put roll top on. Repeat with second pan. Place in oven for 15 minutes or until cheese is melted and it is heated through. Slice along roll lines (to create individual sandwiches) and remove from pan to serve.

Corn Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 cup margarine
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup milk

Directions

Mix together and pour in a 9 x 13 pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Bacon Chestnuts Wrap

Ingredients

  • 1 can whole chestnuts
  • Bacon
  • 1 ½ cups ketchup
  • ¾ cup sugar

Directions

Wrap chestnuts with bacon, bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Mix ketchup and sugar. Pour over baked chestnut/bacon. Bake at 325 degrees for 30 minutes.
