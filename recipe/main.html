Main Dishes

Hawaiian Haystacks

Ingredients

  • Cooked Rices
  • 1 Chicken Breast
  • 2 cans cream of chicken soup
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Oregano

Directions

For the chicken gravy, place the Chicken, soup, and a little bit of paprika, garlic powder, and oregano in a slow cooker and cook on low for 5 hours. Shred after cooked. Cook rice in a pot or rice cooker.

When rice and gravy are finished, place the chicken gravy on the rice and put desired toppings on top! Toppings include: Tomatoes, drained pineapples, bell peppers, crunchy rice noodles, black olives, coconut, and so much more!

Easy Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • Any Teriyaki Sauce
  • Cooked Rice
  • 1 Chicken Breast
  • 1/2 package of stir fry mix veggies

Directions

Cut the chicken into bite-size pieces, and place the pieces and some of the teriyaki sauce in a skillet. Cook thoroughly. Add the veggies to the skillet, and some more teriyaki sauce. Cook until veggies are no longer frozen and bright in color, then take off the heat. Serve on cooked rice.

Hamburger Corn Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 12oz can corn, drained
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • ¾ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp pepper
  • 3 cups medium noodles, cooked and drained
  • 2-3 pieces of bread, buttered.

Directions

Brown ground beef with onion. Add and mix corn, cream of mushroom; cream of chicken; sour cream; salt; pepper. Stir in medium noodles. Pour into a 9x13 pan. Tear bread and sprinkle over top. Bake for 30-35 minutes at 350 degrees.

Sloppy Joes

Ingredients

  • 2 lb hamburger meat
  • ½ tsp sage (optional)
  • 1 Tbsp mustard
  • 1 Can Chicken Gumbo soup
  • 1 small onion (optional)
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 3 Tbsp brown sugar

Directions

Brown hamburger in pan until cooked. Mix in other ingredients. Simmer. Serve on hamburger buns.

Chicken Pillows

Ingredients

  • 1 can cooked chicken breasts, diced
  • 4oz cream cheese
  • ½ cup sliced green onions (optional)
  • Garlic Salt
  • Your favorite gravy

Directions

Mix chicken, cream cheese, onions, and sprinkle in garlic salt. Spoon onto individual crescent rolls which are flat. Fold into pillows. Bake according to the crescent roll package. Serve with gravy if you desire.

Chicken Cordon Bleu Casserole

Ingredients

  • 3 – 4 chicken breasts, cubed
  • Slices of ham
  • Swiss cheese
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • ¼ tsp garlic salt
  • Salt and Pepper
  • ¾ cube butter, melted
  • 1 pkg Ritz crackers
  • ½ tsp parsley
  • ½ tsp paprika

Directions

Place chicken in a 9 x 13 pan. Layer ham and Swiss cheese on top. Mix soup and sour cream together. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic salt. Crush up crackers and mix into melted butter. Sprinkle on top of mixture with paprika and parsley. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink.

Chicken Wraps

Ingredients

  • 3-4 Tyson Chicken strips, fried or grilled
  • Tortilla
  • Toppings

Directions

Cook chicken according to package. Wrap chicken and desired toppings in tortilla. Toppings can include: Bacon, Ham, Lettuce, Tomatoes, Cheese, Avocado, beans, onions, bell peppers, cucumbers, Miracle Whip, Ranch, BBQ sauce, etc.
